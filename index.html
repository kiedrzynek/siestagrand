Where you can find us

Hotel Siesta Grand is situated in the village Hrabušice, 13 km far from Poprad, where it is possible to visit numerous tourist attractions.

Slovak paradise

From the facility, there is a 15-minute walk to Prielom of the Hornád, from where you can perform tourist trips to the gorges or walleyes of The Slovak Paradise (Suchá Belá, Kláštorská roklina, Piecky, Veľký Sokol) and visit Kláštorisko and Tomášovský výhľad.

Spišský hrad

The hotel is also suitable for making trips by car, for visiting cultural and historic monuments of Spiš (Levoča, Spišský hrad /Spiš castle/, Kežmarok), The Gothic Route and natural beauties (Dobšinská ľadová jaskyňa /Dobsina Ice Cave/.

Thermal swimming pool

During the summer you can relax your mind and body in the new thermal complex AquaCity Poprad or in the swimming pool with geothermal water in Vrbov. In winter it is possible to go skiing and cross-country skiing at the distance from 2 to 25 km.
